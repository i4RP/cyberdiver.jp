CORE SYSTEMS

Team Life System

Each team shares a pool of 100,000 life points. Every kill, every Cyber Soul collected drains the enemy's shared life. Zero-sum competition at its finest.

Cyber Souls

Defeated players drop Cyber Souls. Collect enemy souls to deal massive team life damage and heal yourself. Leave them behind at your own risk.

Battle Points

Performance-based scoring: damage dealt, kills, souls collected, survival time. Every action contributes to your Battle Points.

Weapon Arsenal

Handgun, Cyber Rifle, Sniper Rifle. Each weapon with unique stats, fire rates, and tactical advantages. Master them all.

Gate System

Choose your spawn gate (A-E) during the 60-second briefing phase. Strategic positioning starts before the battle begins.

Blockchain Integration

Built on Polygon/MATIC. Zero-sum game model with on-chain verification. Not play-to-earn, but play-to-compete.

THE ARENA

5-minute high-intensity battles in a cyberpunk arena. Neon-lit corridors, strategic cover points, and multiple flanking routes. Every match is a new dive into the cyber battlefield.
